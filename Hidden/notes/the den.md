## Mother Night’s Dreadbound Werewolf

*Large humanoid (shapechanger), chaotic evil*
**CR 10**

**AC** 17 (natural armor)
**HP** 168 (16d10+80)
**Speed** 40 ft.

STR 20 (+5) | DEX 14 (+2) | CON 20 (+5) | INT 10 (+0) | WIS 14 (+2) | CHA 16 (+3)

**Saving Throws** Str +9, Con +9, Wis +6, Cha +7
**Skills** Perception +10, Stealth +6
**Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, slashing from nonmagical attacks
**Damage Immunities** necrotic
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 20
**Languages** Common, Abyssal

---

### Traits

**Shapechanger.** Bonus action to shift between humanoid and hybrid.

**Aura of Nightfire.** Creatures that start their turn within 10 ft take **7 (2d6) necrotic or cold** damage (choice). Nonmagical light is halved.

**Shadow of Mother Night.** Nonmagical light within 20 ft is extinguished. Magical light of 3rd level or lower is suppressed.

**Demonic Regeneration.** Regains **15 HP** at start of its turn unless it took radiant damage since last turn.

**Silver Vulnerability.** Takes **+10 damage** from silvered weapons.

**Blessing of the Huntress.** Advantage on attacks against **prone, frightened, or restrained** creatures.

---

### Actions

**Multiattack.** Two Claws and one Bite.

**Claw.** +9 to hit, 5 ft. Hit: **14 (2d8+5) slashing + 7 (2d6) necrotic**.

**Bite.** +9 to hit, 5 ft. Hit: **18 (2d10+5) piercing + 7 (2d6) necrotic**.
DC 16 Con save or be cursed (lycanthropy or demonic curse).

**Moonfall Leap (Recharge 5–6).** Teleports up to 40 ft. Creatures within 10 ft of landing must make a **DC 16 Dex save** or take **21 (6d6) necrotic** damage and fall prone (half on success).

**Tether of the Black Moon.** +9 to hit, reach 15 ft.
Hit: **15 (2d8+5) slashing + 10 (3d6) necrotic**, DC 16 Str save or pulled 10 ft, knocked prone, and **restrained by shadow** until end of target’s next turn.

**Mother Night’s Judgment (Recharge 6).** Enemies of choice within 30 ft make a **DC 16 Wis save** or take **14 (4d6) psychic** damage and be **frightened** for 1 minute (save at end of turns).

---

### Reactions

**The Hunt Does Not End.** When a creature leaves its reach, make a Claw attack. On hit, target’s speed becomes **0** and it takes **10 necrotic** damage.

---

### Phase Change

**Dark Moon Ascendant (Once, at 50% HP).**
For 1 minute:

* Aura of Nightfire becomes **15 ft**
* Gains **resistance to radiant**
* End of its turn: one prone or frightened creature within 15 ft takes **7 necrotic** damage
