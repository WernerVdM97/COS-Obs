# Flameskulls
## **Khazan (failed lich aspirant)**

- “The path… the path was mine. But the fire took me first.”
    
- “I was _so close_. Give me your soul—I can finish it this time.”
    
- “The lich… the lich… why isn’t it me?”
    
- “I remember a tower… and then nothing but flame.”
    

---

## **Arcturus the Binder (jailer of vestiges)**

- “Step back. The prisoners sleep. Do not wake them.”
    
- “Amber holds the dead gods. Amber holds _you_, if you’re foolish.”
    
- “Bindings… failing… must burn the weakness out.”
    
- “You don’t understand what scratches inside that stone.”
    

---

## **Maelra the Archivist (scholar of forbidden lore)**

- “Name them correctly, or they will name _you_.”
    
- “So many pages, burnt away… just like me.”
    
- “Your thoughts are loud. Stop thinking about the sarcophagus.”
    
- “Give me your book. I can taste the ink… yes… yes…”
    

---

## **Vadrik the Gatewarden (warden of wards)**

- “Fault detected. You are the fault.”
    
- “Lines disrupted. Wards flickering. Burn the intrusion.”
    
- “Do not move. I must recalculate the geometry of your bones.”
    
- “Everything is broken. Everything must be corrected by fire.”
