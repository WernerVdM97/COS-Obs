## Vampiric Curse

**Creature Type:** Humanoid (Vampire)  
**Alignment:** unchanged

### Traits

- **Darkvision:** 60 ft (or +30 ft if already has it)
    
- **Vampiric Resilience:** resistance to necrotic damage
    
- **Sunlight Sensitivity:** disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks relying on sight while in sunlight
    
- **Forbiddance:** cannot enter a residence without invitation
    

### Hunger

- At dawn, make a **DC 13 Wisdom save**
    
    - **Fail:** disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks until you feed
        
    - **Feed:** drain blood from a willing creature or helpless humanoid (1 minute)
        

### Blood Drinker

- Once per turn when you deal melee damage, regain HP equal to your **proficiency bonus**
