**Blessing of Mother Night.** Immune to divination magic (_nondetection_ effect) and resistant to harmful magic (implied by high AC and saves) 815.

**Spellcasting.** 16th-level wizard (Int-based; DC 17, +9 spell attacks).

### **Tactics**
